Welcome Families

At Little Hearts Learning Daycare, children are cared for in a loving home environment where they can feel safe, supported, and excited to learn each day.

Our goal is to provide more than supervision. We provide structure, comfort, play, learning activities, outdoor time, meals, rest, and daily communication with families.

What We Offer

Safe Home-Based Care

A clean, welcoming, child-focused space designed for comfort, supervision, and daily routines.

Learning Through Play

Story time, educational activities, creative play, outdoor play, and hands-on learning experiences.

Meals & Snacks

Meals and snacks are included, with family communication about your child’s daily needs.

Family Communication

Parents are welcome to call or text, and updates are shared so families feel connected.
