About Little Hearts

At Little Hearts Learning Daycare, children are cared for in a safe, loving, and structured environment. Our goal is to help each child feel known, supported, and excited to learn through play, routines, story time, creativity, and hands-on activities.

Currently preparing for licensing. Enrollment interest is welcome while licensing is being completed.

Our Program

Learning Through Play

Daily activities encourage creativity, language, problem solving, sharing, and confidence.

Daily Routine

Children benefit from a steady routine with meals, rest, outdoor play, learning time, and free play.

Outdoor Fun

Outdoor activities may include bubbles, gardening, chalk, sensory play, story time, and safe active play.

Meals & Snacks

Breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snack are planned to support healthy growth and happy days.
