Your Peace of Mind

Health & Safety

❤️

CPR & First Aid Certified

Caregivers are trained to respond quickly and calmly in medical situations involving infants and children.

🛡️

Background Checked Staff

Caregivers complete background checks through California Department of Justice and FBI requirements before working with children.

👶

Safe Sleep Practices

Infants are placed on their backs in a firm, flat sleep space, free of loose bedding and soft objects.

🔒

Secure Drop-Off & Pick-Up

Children are released only to authorized adults listed on the enrollment form. Photo ID may be required.

🌡️

Illness Policy

Children with fever of 100.4°F or higher, vomiting, diarrhea, or contagious illness must stay home.

⚠️

Emergency Preparedness

We practice fire and earthquake procedures and maintain an emergency disaster plan.

Questions Parents Ask

FAQ

What ages do you accept?

We care for children from 4 months through 10 years old.

Are meals included?

Yes. Breakfast, lunch, morning snack, and afternoon snack are included in tuition.

When is payment due?

Payment is due every Monday morning before care begins.

What is your sick child policy?

Children must stay home with fever of 100.4°F or higher, vomiting, diarrhea, contagious rash, pink eye, or other communicable illness symptoms.

How do you communicate with parents?

Text message is the primary method for updates and urgent notifications. Email is used for longer communications and documents. Brightwheel may also be used for daily updates.
