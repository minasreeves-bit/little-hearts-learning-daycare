Meet Mina
“Every parent deserves to focus on their day knowing their child is safe, loved, and growing.”

Mina's journey with children began in her home country of Indonesia, where she spent over a decade working with young learners in international schools and English language courses.

Her passion for early childhood education led her to open her own learning center — nurturing curious minds and building confident communicators from the ground up.

After moving to the United States, Mina brought that same warmth, dedication, and expertise with her. She founded Little Hearts Learning Daycare with one clear mission: to create a safe, joyful space where every child feels celebrated and every family feels at home.

As a mother herself, Mina knows firsthand the trust it takes to leave your child in someone else's care. That's why she holds herself to the highest standard — because at Little Hearts, your child isn't just enrolled. They're family.
